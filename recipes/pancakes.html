Pancakes

It truly does feel like making a cake, but without the oven. The creamy syrup is an extra topping when I have time, but it always tastes good! :D

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups of Flour
  • 2 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of Baking Powder
  • 1 ¼ cups of Milk or Non-Dairy Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 5 tablespoons of Butter
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the butter: Melt it and set it aside to cool for a bit.
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a medium bowl.
  3. Whisk the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Combine wet ingredients: whisk the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract in a separate bowl.
  5. Create a well in the center of the mixture of the dry ingredients and pour the milk misture in.
  6. Gently whisk until the flour is incorporated and looks like a thick batter
  7. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  8. Brush the skillet with melted butter.
  9. Scoop the batter onto the skillet using a ¼ cup measure.
  10. Gently spread each pancake into a 4-inch circle.
  11. After 1 or 2 minutes, the edges will look dry and bubbles will form on the surface so flip the pancake.
  12. Cook for another 1 to 2 minutes until they are lightly browned.
  13. Enjoy!
